Apple Bavarian Torte (★★★★)

A classic European torte baked in a springform pan.Cream cheese, sliced almonds, and apples make this the perfect holiday treat (12 serving).

INGREDIENTS

  • ½ cup butter
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup all- purpose flour
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ½ teaspoon vanila extract
  • ^3/4 cup white sugar
  • 6 apples - peeled,cored,and sliced
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ cup sliced almonds
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup white sugar

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Cream together butter, sugar, vanilla, and flour.
  3. Press crusr mixture into the flat bottom of a 9-inches spingfrom pan. Set aside.
  4. In medium bowl, blend cream cheese and sugar. Beat in egg and vanila. Pour cheese mixture over crust.
  5. toss apples with sugar and cinammon, spread apple mixture over all.
  6. Bake for 10 mintues. Reduce heat to 400 F (200 C) and continue baking for 25 mintues.
  7. Sprinkle almonds over top of torte. Continue baking until lightly browned. Cool before removing from pan.
