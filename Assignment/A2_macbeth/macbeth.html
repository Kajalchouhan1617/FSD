ACT 1

SCENE 1

Summary A thunderstrom approaches andthree witches convene. They agree to confront the great scot general Macbeth upon his victories return from a war between Scotland and Norway. Soon, heroic Macbeth will receive the title of Thane of Cawdor from King Duncan.However, Macbeth learns from the witvhes that is fated for grater things and he will be led down the path of destruction by his unquenchable ambition.
